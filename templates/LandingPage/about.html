What We Offer

System Features

🔐

Secure Authentication

Students and staff log in with their institutional ID. Accounts are verified and protected.

📋

Sit-In Tracking

Real-time logging of student sit-in sessions per lab room with time-in and time-out records.

📊

Admin Dashboard

Administrators get a full overview of active sessions, student history, and usage analytics.

📈

Reports & Analytics

Generate detailed reports on lab usage, peak hours, and per-student attendance records.

🖥️

Lab Management

Track which labs are available, occupied, or under maintenance at any given time.

🔔

Notifications

Students and admins receive alerts for session limits, announcements, and lab updates.

The College

College of Computer Studies

The College of Computer Studies (CCS) at the University of Cebu is dedicated to producing competent and globally competitive IT professionals. With multiple state-of-the-art computer laboratories, the college supports hundreds of students every semester.

This monitoring system was developed to address the growing need for a structured, digital approach to managing sit-in sessions — replacing outdated manual logbooks with a modern, accessible web application.
